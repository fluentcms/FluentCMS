Traditional CMS platforms limit customization, integration, and personalization. This restricts developers and marketers from creating engaging user experiences.

The ideal solution would be a modern, headless CMS that leverages AI for streamlined content creation and management, with a site builder for easy page creation. This would provide developers and marketers with the tools they need to create personalized, engaging experiences for their users, while also being flexible and customizable.
